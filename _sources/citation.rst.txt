#####################
Citing
#####################

If you use OpenPNM in your work, we'd appreciate it if you could
acknowledge OpenPNM by citing the following publication:

.. note::

    Gostick J, Aghighi M, Hinebaugh J, Tranter T, Hoeh MA, Day H,
    Spellacy B, Sharqawy MH, Bazylak A, Burns A, Lehnert W.
    **OpenPNM: a pore network modeling package**.
    `Computing in Science & Engineering. 2016 May 25;18(4):60-74`.
